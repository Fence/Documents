Windows 10 puts notifications and quick actions in action center right on the taskbar where you can get to them instantly
To open action center from the taskbar select the action center icon You can also swipe in from the right edge of your screen or press Windows logo key A
Notifications at a glance If a notification has an arrow to the right select the arrow to read more or take action like replying to a message without having to open the related app or other notification sender
Don t want to take any action
Clear the notification by selecting and dragging it off screen to the right or by selecting the clear button
Want to see notifications from some apps before others in action center
Press and hold or right click a notification group s name and select Make this group high priority
Notifications from high priority senders always appear above notifications from normal priority senders
Take a shortcut Quick actions take you right to the settings and apps you re likely to use often from Bluetooth to brightness
When you open action center you ll see all your available quick actions
Collapse them to see only your top picks
