Great mornings begin with warm freshly baked scones coupled with tea or coffee
Scones are also perfect for afternoon snacks or tea time
They are actually pretty much like the American buttermilk biscuits except in this simplified recipe we ll be using regular milk
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 2 tsp
baking soda
1 cup milk
1 8 cup white sugar
1 tsp
cream of tartar
1 4 cup butter or margarine
A pinch of salt Baking sheet
Parchment paper
Sifter
Mixing bowl
Rolling pin
Basting brush
Wire rack Once your scones are done cool them briefly on wire rack
Serve warm with butter jam clotted cream and maybe fruits and of course some nicely brewed English tea
