HOW TO MAKE CHICKEN FLORENTINE THE EASY WAY Be a chef in your own kitchen
Follow these simple steps and wow your guests with this recipe
PREPARATION TIME
30 minutes WHAT YOU WILL NEED
4 pieces chicken breast fillets
2 cups of bacon bits
4oz of mushroom
200g baby spinach
Half cup of grated mozzarella cheese
1 garlic clove crushed
Lemon juice
Half cup of cream
Salt and pepper for seasoning STEPS
1
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper to taste and place it in the oven and bake for 20 to 30 minutes or until it turns into a nice brown color
2
Remove the chicken breasts from the oven and continue to preheat to 400 degrees so it is ready for a later stage in the process
3
Put some butter in a pan over medium heat
Add in your mushroom soup garlic lemon juice cream seasoning and grated cheese and stir together until everything is well mixed
Remove from heat and set aside
4
On a baking tray cover the base with spinach and add the mushrooms on top of it
Take half of the mixture you have prepared and pour it over the spinach and mushrooms and then place the chicken breasts
Use the rest of the mixture to cover the chicken
Sprinkle it with bacon bits and mozzarella cheese just enough to give it a reasonable coating
5
Put in the preheated oven and bake for another 25 minutes or until the top has turned into a lovely golden brown in color
Remove from oven and serve in a platter
Enjoy
This is how you prepare Chicken Florentine with no hassle
Try it for yourself and serve it to your guests who will undoubtedly love the end result
This dish makes four servings
