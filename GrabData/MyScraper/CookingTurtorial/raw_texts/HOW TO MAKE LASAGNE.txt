This classic Italian dish is filled with the tangy goodness of ground meat vegetables and a lusciously creamy cheese sauce base that oozes delectably in your mouth in between bites
Though lasagne uses a lot of ingredients the procedure for making it is simpler than you may think
Try it today and get a lot of amore from your loved ones
Buon appetito
1 pound lean ground beef or chicken
2 cans 28 ounces each diced or crushed Italian tomatoes
1 2 Tbsp
tomato paste
2 cloves garlic crushed and chopped
1 large onion chopped
1 red bell pepper chopped
1 2 Tbsp
fresh basil coarsely chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1 2 tsp
dried oregano
1 pound lasagne noodles
1 pound mozzarella cheese grated
1 pound ricotta cheese grated
1 2 pound parmesan cheese grated Large saucepan or skillet
Large pot
1 13 9 baking pan
Oven Allow the lasagne to rest for a few minutes before serving
